Step 1 : Create a Product

Create a Product on your Stripe Dashboard. You can optionally add an image of the product that will be visible to your end customers when they click the payment link.

Step 2 : Retrieve your SKU ID.

View all your Products on Stripe here. Select the product for which you'd like to generate a payment link. The Product ID will be visible just below the description

Paste your SKU ID (sku_abcd1234) in the form at the bottom of the page and come back for Step 3.

Step 3 : Retrieve your Publishable Key.

Your Stripe keys are available here. Only copy your Publishable key and paste it in the form at the bottom of the page and come back for Step 4. Do not share your Secret Key with anyone!!

Step 4 : Whitelist this website

Navigate to your Checkout Settings
1. Enable Checkout client-only integration by clicking the slider
2. Copy {{site.url}} to the Domains field
